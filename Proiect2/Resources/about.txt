Description and Game Rules:

-Player with the red pieces always goes first
-Pieces can only move diagonally
-if one piece gets to the last row on the other side, it becomes King 
-King pieces can move forard and backward
-If one piece is captured and there is a possibilty to capture another, multiple jumps can be done
